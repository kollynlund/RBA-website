How We are making a difference in mental health

Our first project with The Cook Center for Human Connection is ParentGuidance.org, which you can find more info about here: ParentGuidance.org.

In addition, we partner with existing organizations who are making a postive impact in mental health. Some examples are wellness coaching, in person or out patient clinics, addiction recovery, homeless shelters, war veteran mentoring and more.

Clear Vision

Clients can visually see their progress. See the bigger picture while still working on the day to day tasks at hand.

Focus on Needs

Coaches/therapist can quickly see where their clients need the most help.

Accountability

Coaches/therapists have access to clients Roadmap to follow up on assignments and action items.

"I love the idea of having a more visual way to evaluate our progress. It allows us to see more objectively the areas where we have made improvement (to feel proud of ourselves) and the areas where we need improvement to address more specifically." - MyRoadmap User

"I like that I can go back to it when I need reassurance. Along the way, we struggle to be confident about ourselves. It is very easy to think that we are stuck.
