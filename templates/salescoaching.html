Have strong coaching engagements to boost sales.

The patent pending MyRoadmap Engagement Platform™ is a formalized and structured methodology to measure and improve the personal engagement between a sales manager and their reps. It helps hold both the rep and his/her manager accountable by facilitating learning, feedback, communication, and engagement.

In a world where managers are skeptical about coaching, you as an organization need to provide a framework for coaching that isn’t over engineered and that doesn’t require them to dramatically change their behavior. It shouldn't require a bunch of hoops, training, or administration. It should provide starter questions to help facilitate coaching discussions.

Problems we see

Focus on “spreadsheet coaching”

This is where managers are only focused on business outcomes like conversion rate, leads generated, deals closed, etc. Coaching is about behaviors, not outcomes.

Lack of Vision

Coaches and sales professionals are not on the same page in understanding exactly what is expected of them or what “good” looks like.

Implementing trainings

Sales professionals struggle to implement the training their managers deliver.

Results from sales coaching

Skills

Coaching your core performers will have a tremendous impact on their skills.

Retention

Good coaching is a major factor in team retention. Good coaches make people want to stay. Bad coaching drives people away.

Effort

Coaching is a major factor in “discretionary” or extra effort. Bad coaches make people want to give up.
