Where we came from

  • In 2015 co-founder Spencer Rogers started the Royal Business Academy, a business school in Prague. Spencer noticed his students received conflicting advice from mentors on how to grow their businesses. Spencer developed "The Roadmap" platform to give his students a clear path on how to be successful.
  • Co-founder Kollyn Lund was a student at The Royal Business Academy and began helping with "The Roadmap" software while growing his own company called QuickStove. As the two built MyRoadmap software they were surprised by the amount of interest from other schools and organizations looking to solve the same issues they encountered.
  • There was so much interest in the software that Spencer and Kollyn decided to go full-time. The dynamic duo officially launched their SaaS company called MyRoadmap in 2017 to deliver the benefits of platform to organizations worldwide.
