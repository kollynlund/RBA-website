What is MyRoadmap?

MyRoadmap is a software platform focused on helping people assess their progress towards and reach personal or professional goals. We do this by (1) defining a clear path (Roadmap); (2) providing a mechanism for self-assessment and accountability to a coach or mentor; and (3) increasing engagement with online content and support.
Kollyn Lund, CEO
