Bill Woahn

CEO

Before MyRoadmap, Bill Woahn served as Chairman of the Board at DriveSafety Inc. and also as its CEO. Mr. Woahn served as CEO of LexisNexis Risk & Information Analytics Solutions, Inc., and The Consumer Advocate Group. He served as President of DriveSafety Inc. He served as Director of Planning for Pactel Communications Systems. He has over 21 years of management experience in technology companies, 10 of which has been in the personal lines insurance industry. He was Owner of Resource Systems Inc., and served as its Partner. Mr. Woahn serves as a Director of Arteis Inc. He holds a Bachelors of Science degree in Civil Engineering and an MBA from Brigham Young University.
